About SVKM

Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal (SVKM) is a renowned Public Charitable Trust, registered under the Societyâ€™s Registration Act and the Bombay Public Trust Act. Since its humble beginnings in 1934, when it took over the Rashtriya Shala, established during the National Movement in 1921, SVKM has grown into a premier educational complex, imparting quality education to over 35,000 students annually.

SVKM stands as a beacon of higher education in India, earning national recognition for its excellence across multiple educational streams. With a strong commitment to holistic development, SVKM fosters an environment where students are not only academically enriched but also equipped with life skills, making them employable and successful in their careers.

About SVKM's IOT, Dhule: The Dhule campus is rapidly emerging as a future hub for technology and education. Strategically located at the junction of three major national highways (NH-3, NH-6, and NH-211), Dhule is poised to transform into an industrial town, driving advancements across infrastructure sectors. SVKM, under the visionary leadership of its president, aims to make Dhule a hub for quality education. The campus, spread over 33 acres, houses state-of-the-art engineering and pharmacy institutes, including SVKM's Institute of Technology, ensuring students receive world-class education in a dynamic, nurturing environment.
