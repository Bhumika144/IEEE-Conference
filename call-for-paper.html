Call for Papers

The IEEE Conference 2025 invites researchers, scholars, and industry professionals to submit original, unpublished research contributions in the following areas:

Topics of Interest

  • Wireless Communications and Networking
  • Signal Processing and Multimedia
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Applications
  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence
  • Embedded Systems and Robotics
  • Cybersecurity and Privacy

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: January 10, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: February 10, 2025
  • Final Camera-Ready Submission: February 28, 2025

All accepted papers will be published in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library. We look forward to your contributions and participation!
