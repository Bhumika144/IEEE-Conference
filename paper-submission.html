Paper Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original research papers through the conference submission portal. Please ensure your paper adheres to the IEEE format:

Submission Guidelines

  • Papers should be written in English and not exceed 6 pages (including figures and references).
  • Use the IEEE conference template for formatting (Download Template).
  • Ensure your paper is plagiarism-free and has not been submitted elsewhere.

Submission Portal

Submit your paper through the official submission portal: EasyChair Submission.

For any submission-related queries, please contact us at papers@ieeeconf2025.org.
