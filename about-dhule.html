Dhule, located in northwestern Maharashtra, is a vibrant blend of rich heritage, scenic landscapes, and modern development. Surrounded by lush hills and fertile plains, it's not just known for its natural beauty but also for its growing industrial and educational prominence. Strategically positioned at the junction of NH-3, NH-6, and NH-211, Dhule has emerged as a hub for industries and top-tier education. Among its proud institutions is SVKM's Institute of Technology, a cutting-edge campus offering quality education in engineering and pharmacy. With its cultural charm and future-ready infrastructure, Dhule truly bridges the gap between tradition and innovation.
